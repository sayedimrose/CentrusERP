# Centrus ERP: Modules Sitemap

The sitemap defines the navigation structure of the Next.js frontend application, ensuring logical grouping for ease of use across different business operations.

- **Dashboard**
  - KPI Overview (Sales, Expenses, Profit)
  - Pending Approvals

- **Point of Sale (POS)**
  - Open/Close Session
  - Main POS Interface (Sales Register)
  - Return / Refund
  - Daily Z-Report

- **Sales**
  - Customers
  - Quotations
  - Sales Orders
  - Sales Invoices (ZATCA compliant)
  - Credit Notes
  - **Reports**
    - Item-wise Sales Price
    - Sales Register & Summaries
    - Customer Statements

- **Purchases**
  - Suppliers
  - Purchase Orders
  - Goods Receipts (GRN)
  - Purchase Invoices
  - Debit Notes
  - **Reports**
    - Item Vendor Report (Vendor Cost Prices)
    - Vendor Cost & Sales Comparison
    - Item Cost & Sales Comparison
    - Purchase Analysis (Grouping & Filtering)

- **Inventory**
  - Products & Services
  - Product Categories & Brands
  - Warehouses & Sub-Locations (Primary, Damage, Custom)
  - Stock Transfers
  - Stock Adjustments
  - Damage & Expiry Logging
  - Consumables Issue
  - **Reports**
    - Item-wise Stock Report
    - Vendor-wise Stock Report
    - Inventory Valuation
    - Wastage & Expiry Logs

- **Manufacturing**
  - Bill of Materials (BOM)
  - Work Centers
  - Production Orders
  - **Reports**
    - Production Yield & Variance
    - BOM Costing Analysis

- **Finance & Accounts**
  - Chart of Accounts
  - Journal Entries
  - General Ledger
  - Accounts Payable (AP) / Accounts Receivable (AR)
  - Bank Accounts & Reconciliation
  - Payments & Receipts (Vouchers)
  - Taxes (ZATCA / VAT Settings)
  - **Reports**
    - Bank Statements
    - Active Vendor Payments & AP Aging
    - Profit & Loss
    - Balance Sheet
    - Trial Balance
    - Tax Return Report

- **Human Resources**
  - Employees (Iqama, Passports tracking)
  - Attendance
  - Leave Applications
  - Payroll & Salary Slips
  - **Reports**
    - Document Expiry Alerts
    - Payroll Registers

- **Settings & Configuration**
  - Company Profile (VAT Number, Logo)
  - Branch Setup
  - Users & Roles
  - Payment Modes Configuration (Enable/Disable toggles)
  - Hardware Devices (Printers, Scanners)
  - ZATCA Phase 2 Integration Settings
  - General Preferences (Currency, Timezone)
